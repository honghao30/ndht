Measuring Equipment

Our precision measurement solutions provide high-accuracy and high-efficiency systems optimized for industrial environments, from non-contact real-time scanners to digital calipers and micrometers. With data transmission and Bluetooth connectivity, they enable accurate measurements and instant analysis for a smart measurement experience.
Real-Time Scanning
A non-contact optical measurement device designed for camshafts and shafts, capable of screw measurements, and allows for instant reporting using real-time Reflex Scan software.
2D Measurement Device
An exceptionally durable 2D measurement device that allows data transmission and enables various measurements with probe replacement.
Digital Vernier Calipers
Equipped with specialized caliper solutions, especially suitable for production environments with micron-level calipers, Bluetooth, and waterproof functionality.
Digital Indicators
Available in both inch and metric units, offering a wide range of measurement capabilities, from mini sizes to large measuring ranges.
Digital Bore Gauges
Easy measurement with a gun-type handle and convenient data transfer with Bluetooth functionality.
Digital Micrometers
Offers rapid measurement speeds with a speed spindle and non-rotating micrometer, reducing spindle wear.
Digital Filler Gauge
The first digital filler gauge.The display shows the combined thickness of all blades use dmm/inch blade selection is available. Data transmission via Bluetooth is possible.
Platform
The platform is a product that allows for precision measurement of internal or external dimensions in industrial settings. It can significantly measure extremely small parts or products that are too heavy and fragile.
Software
We provide a program that enhances user convenience with duplex communication (two-way) that allows data transmission, collection, and output.
